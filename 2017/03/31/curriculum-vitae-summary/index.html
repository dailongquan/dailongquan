Curriculum Vitae Summary

Posted on 2017-03-31

Currently Longquan Dai is an Assistant Professor in Intelligent Media Analysis Group (IMAG) at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology（NJUST). He received his B.A., M.S. and Ph.D. degrees from Henan University of Technology (HAUT) in 2006, Shantou Univeristy (STU) in 2010 and National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) in 2016, respetively. Before jointing IMAG, he was a senior engineer of Huawei Technologies Co. Ltd. His Master and Doctoral supervisors are Prof. Minfen Shen who was the dean of the College of Engineering and Prof. Xiaopeng Zhang a member of Sina-European Laboratory of Informatics and Applied Mathematics (LIAMA).

Research interests: Image Processing, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Computational Photography

Email: lqdai at foxmail dot com or dailongquan at njust dot edu dot cn

Address: Room 2079, School of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing Univeristy of Science and Technology, Nanjing, China.
